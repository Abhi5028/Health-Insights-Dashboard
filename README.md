# Global-Health-Insights-Dashboard

## Overview
The **Global Health Insights Dashboard** is a data visualization project designed to analyze global healthcare metrics. It provides a high-level overview of disease prevalence, treatment economics, and healthcare accessibility across different regions and demographics. 

## Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)
The dashboard highlights the following overarching global metrics:
* [cite_start]**Average Treatment Cost:** $25.01K [cite: 13, 16]
* [cite_start]**Average Mortality Rate:** 5% [cite: 12, 15]
* [cite_start]**Healthcare Access:** 75% [cite: 14, 17]

## Core Visualizations & Features

### 1. Disease Impact by Demographics
* [cite_start]**Total Population Affected by Disease:** This chart visualizes the affected population distributed by Gender (Female, Male, Other)[cite: 6, 7]. 
* [cite_start]It covers major disease categories: Autoimmune, Neurological, Viral, Metabolic, Parasitic, Genetic, Infectious, Bacterial, Chronic, Respiratory, and Cardiovascular[cite: 42, 44, 46, 49, 51, 53, 55, 57, 59, 61, 63]. 
* [cite_start]Across these categories, the total population affected consistently registers at approximately 60M[cite: 43, 45, 48, 50, 52, 54, 56, 58, 60, 62, 64].

### 2. Treatment Economics
* [cite_start]**Avg Treatment Cost by Treatment Type:** Compares the financial impact of different healthcare interventions[cite: 31].
* [cite_start]The data covers Medication, Therapy, Surgery, and Vaccination[cite: 35, 36, 39, 40].
* [cite_start]The average cost for each of these treatment types is uniformly distributed at around $25K[cite: 32, 33, 37, 38].

### 3. Global Geospatial Analysis
* [cite_start]**Country and Average Treatment Cost (USD):** A map visual powered by Microsoft Bing that charts out the average treatment costs globally[cite: 18, 19, 21].
* [cite_start]It maps data across major regions including North America, South America, Europe, Africa, and Asia[cite: 20, 24, 25, 26, 29, 30].

### 4. Vaccine Availability
* [cite_start]**Total Population Affected by Vaccine Available?:** A breakdown of populations based on whether a vaccine is available for their condition[cite: 70].
* [cite_start]The data indicates an even split: 50% (20M) with a vaccine available ("Yes") and 50% (20M) without ("No")[cite: 71, 72, 74, 75].

### 5. Healthcare Access & Mortality
* [cite_start]**Healthcare Access By Mortality Rate by Country:** A scatter or matrix visual correlating the sum of mortality rates with the average healthcare access[cite: 77, 80, 88].
* [cite_start]The analysis includes specific country data points such as Argentina, Australia, Brazil, Canada, and China[cite: 79].
